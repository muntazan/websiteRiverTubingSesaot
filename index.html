Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you prepare for your river tubing adventure.

Is it safe for beginners? expand_more
Absolutely! Our river paths are carefully selected, and professional guides accompany you throughout the journey. Premium safety gear is mandatory and provided.
What should I bring? expand_more
We recommend bringing comfortable swimwear or light clothing, a change of clothes, a towel, and water-friendly footwear (sandals or water shoes). We handle all the safety gear.
How long does the adventure take? expand_more
The short track (500m) takes about 30-45 minutes. The popular long track (2.5km) typically takes 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on the river flow and group pace.
Are there age restrictions? expand_more
Participants generally need to be at least 7 years old. For younger children, please contact us in advance to discuss specific arrangements and ensure their safety.
